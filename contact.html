FAQ

Common Questions

How long does the course take?
The program is self-paced and structured progressively. Most students complete the core curriculum within 8–12 weeks with consistent effort.
Do I need any prior trading experience?
No experience required. The course starts from the very foundation of how markets work and builds progressively to advanced concepts.
Is the course taught in Malayalam?
Yes — entirely in Malayalam. All lessons, explanations, and support are delivered in clear, professional Malayalam so nothing is lost in translation.
Will I be able to trade after the course?
You will have the knowledge and system to begin practicing. We always recommend paper trading before risking real capital. Skill requires consistent practice over time.
What about funded trading opportunities?
We are building connections with professional prop firms. Top-performing students may be considered for funding pathways as our ecosystem develops.
How do I enroll?
Simply fill in the form on this page or reach out via WhatsApp. Our team will walk you through the enrollment process and answer any questions.
