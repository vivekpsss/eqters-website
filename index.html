Start With the Basics

Trading Basics

What is
Trading?

Trading is the act of buying and selling financial assets — such as stocks, currencies, or commodities — with the goal of profiting from price movements over time.

Unlike long-term investing, trading involves active participation in markets. A skilled trader builds a system: a clear set of rules for when to enter, when to exit, how much to risk, and how to manage their emotions under pressure.

Done correctly, trading is a craft. Like any craft, it can be learned — and mastered — with the right guidance.
