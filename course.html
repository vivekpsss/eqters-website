Course Overview

What This Program
Is Designed to Do

The Eqters Core Program is a structured educational journey built to give you a genuine, professional understanding of financial market trading.

This is not a collection of random tips or indicators. It is a progressive curriculum — each module building on the last — designed to develop a complete, professional trading skill set.

By the end of this program, you will have a system. Your own strategy, your own rules, and the discipline to follow them.

Curriculum

01

Introduction to Financial Markets

How markets work, who the participants are, and what drives price.

02

Reading Price & Market Structure

Chart reading fundamentals, candlestick analysis, and understanding market phases.

03

Technical Analysis

Key technical tools, support and resistance, trends, and high-probability setups.

04

Risk & Capital Management

Position sizing, risk-reward, stop loss strategy, and protecting your capital.

05

Trading Psychology

The mental game of trading — managing bias, emotions, and building discipline.

06

Building Your Personal Strategy

Define your edge, document your rules, and build a strategy you can trust.
